# Game Setup

Follow these steps to set up the game:

## 1. Choose Faction

* Each player chooses a faction (Tang / Song / Yuan / Ming) and receives:
  * One corresponding Faction Attribute Panel
  * 10 Title Cards from that faction
  * One set of Execute Policy Cards (23 cards)
  * One set of Issue Policy Cards (4 cards)
  * 3 Special Policy Cards from that faction

## Place Attribute Tokens

Each faction's initial attributes are as follows:

| Faction | Military | Livelihood | Wealth | Faith |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tang | 5 | 3 | 4 | 2 |
| Song | 2 | 5 | 4 | 3 |
| Yuan | 5 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
| Ming | 4 | 3 | 5 | 2 |

* Place the four attribute tokens on your faction panel at their starting values

## Set Up Map

* Shuffle Land Tiles and Obstacle Tiles together, then place them in the map spaces in order.
* Land Tiles are face-up, Obstacle Tiles are face-down.

## Distribute Starting Resources

* Each faction receives 5000 coins: 1000 ×2, 500 ×4, 100 ×10

## Player Order

* In historical order: Tang → Song → Yuan → Ming
